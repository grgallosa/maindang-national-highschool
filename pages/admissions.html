Admissions

Join our growing community of learners. Discover how to apply and start your academic journey at Maindang National High School.

Enrollment Process

Follow these simple steps to complete your enrollment at MNHS.

  • 1

    Submit Application Form

    Fill out and submit the official admission form with accurate details. You can get it from the registrar’s office or download it online.

  • 2

    Submit Required Documents

    Provide all necessary documents, including your Form 138, birth certificate, and certificate of good moral character.

  • 3

    Assessment and Interview

    Applicants may undergo an evaluation or interview conducted by the guidance counselor or admissions committee.

  • 4

    Confirmation and Enrollment

    Once accepted, you’ll receive an official confirmation slip. Proceed to the registrar to finalize enrollment.

Admission Requirements

Please prepare the following documents before proceeding to the registrar’s office.

For Junior High School

  • Form 138 (Report Card)
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2 pcs. 1x1 ID Photo (White Background)

For Senior High School

  • Form 138 / SF9
  • Form 137 / SF10
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • 4 pcs. 1x1 ID Photo

For Transferees

  • Transfer Credentials
  • Report Card / Transcript
  • Good Moral Certificate
  • Birth Certificate (PSA)
