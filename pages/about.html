About Maindang National High School

A Beacon of Learning on the Hilltop — Shaping Minds since 2002.

Our Story

Founded in 2002, Maindang National High School (MNHS) stands as a pillar of public secondary education in Cuartero, Capiz. Situated on a scenic hilltop in Barangay Maindang, our school offers a peaceful and inspiring environment for learning.

As part of the Schools Division of Capiz (DepEd Region VI), MNHS empowers youth from our rural community with quality education, discipline, and values that prepare them to face the challenges of the modern world.

Our Core Philosophy

We are guided by our mission, vision, and values — the foundation of our school's identity.

Mission

To deliver quality, inclusive, and values-based education that fosters lifelong learning and community service.

Vision

A leading institution producing competent, compassionate, and responsible individuals for a global society.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Discipline
  • Excellence
  • Service

Academic Programs & Student Life

Beyond the classroom, MNHS fosters holistic growth and leadership.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive K-12 curriculum, including Junior and Senior High School tracks. Senior High School students participate in Work Immersion programs to gain real-world experience.

Organizations

From leadership in the Supreme Student Government to community outreach through YES-O and GSP, we encourage student involvement and service.

Sports

Our annual Intramurals, themed “Unite and Compete,” promote sportsmanship, camaraderie, and physical fitness.
