MNHS Athletes Return to WVRAA with Medal Wins

2023 Sports Program

Maindang National High School proudly celebrates the outstanding performance of its student-athletes at the Capiz Provincial Meet 2023, marking a successful return to the Western Visayas Regional Athletic Association (WVRAA).

Through discipline, perseverance, and rigorous training, three MNHS athletes earned multiple medals, bringing pride and honor to the school and the community.

Medal Winners

  • Reymark Abad — Two Gold Medals
    800-meter Run and 1500-meter Run
  • Ian Aguillon — One Gold Medal
    High Jump
  • Lorence Aleligay — Two Silver Medals
    5000-meter Run and 3000-meter Steeplechase

These achievements reflect the athletes’ dedication to excellence, strong work ethic, and commitment to representing Maindang National High School with integrity and sportsmanship.

Pride of the MNHS Community

Beyond the medals, their success serves as an inspiration to fellow students, demonstrating that perseverance and determination can lead to remarkable accomplishments both on and off the field.

“We are proud of our athletes. Your dedication and hard work have brought honor to MNHS.”
