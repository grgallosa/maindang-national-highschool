MNHS Proudly Honors 2025 Chief Girl Scout Medalists

2025 MNHS Admin

Maindang National High School proudly extends its warmest congratulations to our 2025 Chief Girl Scout Medalists, Senior Girl Scout Lorien Bartocillo Facal and Senior Girl Scout Glaiza Denosta.

The Chief Girl Scout Medal is one of the highest honors conferred by the Girl Scouts of the Philippines. This prestigious recognition is awarded to young women who demonstrate exceptional leadership, dedication to service, and active involvement in community development.

Excellence in Leadership and Service

Senior Girl Scouts Lorien B. Facal and Glaiza P. Denosta have shown unwavering commitment through the successful completion of their projects, consistent participation in Girl Scouting activities, and exemplary conduct as student leaders. Their achievement brings pride and honor to the entire MNHS community.

Guidance and Support

The school sincerely acknowledges Troop Leader Lyn Salazar Enagan for her steadfast guidance, mentorship, and encouragement. Her dedication played a vital role in guiding our Girl Scouts toward this remarkable accomplishment.

We also extend our sincere appreciation to Sir Mark Nel Venus, School Principal, for his continued support and leadership. His commitment to fostering a nurturing and empowering learning environment greatly contributes to the success of MNHS learners.

An Inspiration to the MNHS Community

As these young leaders move forward, may they continue to uphold the values of service, integrity, and responsibility embodied by the Chief Girl Scout Medal. Their journey serves as an inspiration to all learners and a reminder that excellence is achieved through perseverance and commitment.

“From the Hills, We Rise to Lead.”
